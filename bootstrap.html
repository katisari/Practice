California Roll
Michigan roll


California roll is one of the most popular type of sushi that people enjoy. It is usually made from imitation crab, avocado, and cucumber. So why is it called the California roll? The origin of the name comes from people's belief that the sushi was first served at a Japanese restaurant in Los Angeles.


Michigan roll also has avocado like the California roll, but it also has spicy tuna. It isn't globally recognized like the California roll but is served in local restuarants in Michigan.

The good: For non meat lovers this is for you!

The good: The meat is real!

The bad: Most California Roll's crab meat is fake.

The bad: Not adequate for vegetarians.
